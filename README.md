# Sorghu-Shield: Innovative & Cost-Effective Eco-Friendly Food Packaging

## 🌱 Overview
Sorghu-Shield is an eco-friendly food packaging solution developed using sweet sorghum bagasse. It aims to replace plastic containers with biodegradable, cost-effective, and durable alternatives suitable for real-world food applications.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement
- Plastic packaging causes severe environmental pollution  
- Existing paper/bagasse containers fail with hot food (leakage, softening)  
- Lack of strong, affordable biodegradable alternatives  

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution
- Use sweet sorghum bagasse fiber  
- Manufacture leak-proof and durable food containers  
- Provide a sustainable alternative to plastic packaging  

---

## 🚀 Innovation & Creativity
- Novel use of sweet sorghum fiber  
- High-temperature hot pressing technology  
- Leak-proof design for Indian food (curries, gravies)  
- Fully biodegradable and compostable  

---

## 🌾 Raw Material Details
- Material: Sweet Sorghum Stalk (outer rind)  
- Growth period: 120–135 days  
- Moderate water requirement  
- Availability: Andhra Pradesh  

---

## ⚙️ Manufacturing Process
Raw Material → Cleaning → Crushing → Pulping → Molding → Vacuum Dewatering → Hot Pressing → Trimming → Sterilization → Packing  

---

## 🏭 Process Explanation
- **Pulping:** Converts fiber into slurry  
- **Forming:** Creates shape using molds  
- **Hot Pressing:** Provides strength and smooth finish  
- **Sterilization:** Ensures food safety  

---

## ✅ Advantages
- Eco-friendly and biodegradable  
- Leak-proof and heat resistant  
- Low production cost  
- Lightweight but strong  
- High recyclability (90–95%)  
- Suitable for mass production
